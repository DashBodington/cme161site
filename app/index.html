Random Triangle Meshes

This visualization is of random points being generated and plotted. At each time step, a random combination of the point's last value and a another random number is computed and plotted. The visualization therefore waxes and wanes randomly. Thin sections indicate that the value at the last timestep had a greater weight in the random combination, while thick sections indicate that randomness played a greater role. The library used is processing.js. (This example was made by Dave Deriso.)
